一、概述

栖阅（QiReader）是一款运行在 iOS / iPadOS / macOS 上的阅读客户端。我们高度重视用户隐私，本政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

二、信息收集与使用

栖阅 不会 收集、存储或传输任何个人身份信息。

本应用唯一涉及网络的隐私相关功能为 iCloud 同步：

  • iCloud 同步：当您开启 iCloud 同步功能后，您的书架数据、阅读进度、书源配置等信息会通过 Apple 的 iCloud（CloudKit）服务在您的设备间同步。这些数据存储在您的个人 iCloud 账户中，由 Apple 管理和保护，我们无法访问。

除 iCloud 同步外，本应用：

  • 不注册或要求任何用户账户
  • 不收集设备标识、位置信息或使用分析数据
  • 不包含任何第三方追踪器或广告 SDK
  • 不向任何第三方共享用户数据

三、本地数据

所有阅读数据（书架、阅读进度、应用设置等）默认存储在您的设备本地。您可以随时在应用设置中管理和删除这些数据。

四、网络请求

本应用在以下情况会发起网络请求：

  • 使用用户自行导入的书源获取书籍内容
  • 开启 iCloud 同步时与 Apple iCloud 服务器通信

上述请求均由用户主动触发，不涉及任何隐性数据收集。

五、儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童设计，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

六、隐私政策更新

我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在此页面发布，并注明新的生效日期。

七、联系方式

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：

  • GitHub：https://github.com/michlinanjir1jsy/QiReader

English Version

1. Overview

QiReader is a reading client for iOS / iPadOS / macOS. The Chinese app name is Qiyue. We take user privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information Collection and Use

QiReader does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

The only privacy-related network feature is iCloud Sync:

  • iCloud Sync: When you enable iCloud sync, your bookshelf data, reading progress, and book source configurations are synced across your devices via Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account, managed and protected by Apple. We have no access to it.

Other than iCloud sync, this app:

  • Does not require any user registration or account
  • Does not collect device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics
  • Does not include any third-party trackers or advertising SDKs
  • Does not share any user data with third parties

3. Local Data

All reading data (bookshelf, reading progress, app settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device by default. You can manage and delete this data at any time through the app settings.

4. Network Requests

This app makes network requests in the following cases:

  • Fetching book content using book sources imported by the user
  • Communicating with Apple's iCloud servers when iCloud sync is enabled

All requests are initiated by user actions and involve no implicit data collection.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
